Study Sessions

In Study World, we have study sessions where students all over the world can join and work on their studies together.

Anyone can start a study session in several voice channels (muted and unmuted channels). This way everyone can focus on their studies/projects while studying together with other students!

You may study in silence, which most people prefer, but you can also turn on your camera or share your screen and show us what you're working on.

School Help

With so many members all studying different courses, you'll be able to get help with any work you have!

Just remember: We will not help you if you are in an exam!


Server Features

In Study World, we have a custom bot called Legolas, developed by JonathanGr#1650. It posts motivational quotes once a day, sends welcome messages and has user interactions commands like hug and wave.

We have an exp system with Legolas, including custom designed rank cards.
