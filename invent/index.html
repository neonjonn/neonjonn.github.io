Irish Sign Language (ISL)

The Irish Sign Language is called ISL. There are only about 40,000 hearing users in Ireland, thats only about 0.8% of Ireland's hearing population. We came up with the idea of a device that could translate ISL into different languages. For our prototype we have attached the micro:bit to the thumb, and it should be able to differentiate between the letters B and L.
